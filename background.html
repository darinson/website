Background

My name is Darin Son, or 손다린.
I grew up in Seoul, South Korea.
I attended Seoul International School, where I was a varsity swimmer and online newspaper editor.
I moved to Irvine, California with my family in 2015, after I graduated high school.
I attended Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo for my undergraduate and graduate studies in Environmental Engineering. As an undergraduate, I also worked as a part-time technical intern for the Cal Poly Facilities department and a sustainability intern for University Housing.
After defending my master's thesis in September 2020, I have been searching for jobs, working part-time as a data analyst and exploring the data science field -- amongst other things in Life!
